Who is this Programme for?

The Digital and Social Media Marketing programme provides experiential learning in digital marketing strategy and social media marketing strategy through diverse case-studies, exposure to emerging technologies, latest trends & best practices, industry speaker sessions, a compulsory project-work guided by IIMC faculty and an industry mentor, digital marketing and social media marketing simulations, and face-to-face learning during campus immersions.

This programme is carefully created for:

  • Mid-career Professionals and Consultants keen on driving digital growth and customer acquisition strategies by leveraging digital opportunities, analytics and emerging technologies to meet business outcomes, such as elevating brand awareness, enhancing brand recall, driving customer acquisition and augmenting customer experience.
  • Early Professionals and Entrepreneurs looking to advance their capabilities in digital and social media marketing to successfully plan, implement and measure digital growth strategy and contribute to business sales and profitability.
  • Senior Executives who wish to develop strategic skills in digital and social media marketing to deliver exponential growth of digital consumers will also benefit from the programme.

Project

All participants must do a compulsory project based on a topic of their choice related to digital and/or social media marketing. Non-completion of this project would lead to an incomplete grade in the programme. Each project would be guided by an Ready Accountantfaculty along with an industry mentor to be chosen by the participant him/herself. The project would be graded separately by the faculty guide and industry mentor.

Programme Certificate

Participants will be awarded a Certificate of Completion from Ready Accountanton successfully attending at least 66% in each module and obtaining no more than two ‘Poor’ grades in the entire programme. Participants with requisite attendance but more than two ‘Poor’ grades will receive a Certificate of Participation. Failure to submit assessments/ simulations/ take an examination will result in the participant receiving an ‘incomplete’ status and no certificate will be awarded. A participant without requisite attendance in every module will not be awarded a certificate.

Prof. Ramendra Singh

Professor, Marketing,

IIM Calcutta

Prof. Ramendra Singh is currently the Professor of Marketing at IIM Calcutta. He has a PhD (IIM Ahmedabad), MBA (XLRI Jamshedpur), and B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (IIT-BHU). His academic research has been published in reputed international journals including, Journal of Business Research, Journal of Macro Marketing, International Marketing Review, among others. He serves on Editorial Boards of several leading international academic journals, guest edited several special issues, and has written several articles in business publications. His research interests include marketing and the poor, ICT for Development, business and society, and salesforce performance management. He has authored several books, including an edited book titled, Bottom of Pyramid Marketing: Making, Shaping and Developing BOP Markets, a textbook on Sales and Distribution Management, and two books on marketing case studies.
